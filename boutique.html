NUPUR DHARIWAL - DESIGN STUDIO

Fashion is one of the most significant aspects of culture in both a historical and social context. It speaks to intentions, values, status, and tastes, and functions like a language as old as time. Evening wear is one of the most prominent occurrences in which the importance of fashion reveals itself. Whether the occasion is a wedding, a gala, the opera, or prom, there are numerous events that call for the formal attire of ball gowns and black ties. Fashion becomes a vital outlet of expression in these socially significant circumstances.
